The service

A simple service for people who want a garden without figuring out everything alone.

We keep the service focused: check the space, recommend a setup, install it, and offer maintenance later if needed.

01

Check the space

We look at size, sunlight, water access, and whether normal or hydroponic setup makes sense.

02

Install the garden

We bring the materials, install the growing area, prepare the setup, and start the first planting.

03

Support after setup

You can add maintenance for care visits, replanting, troubleshooting, and harvest support.

Pricing

Pricing follows size and setup type.

All setup prices include materials, installation, and initial setup. Final quote depends on actual size, access, and selected setup type.

Normal setup

Best for soil-based pots, planter boxes, and simple raised beds. Lower material cost and easier for most homes.

Hydroponic setup

Best for cleaner systems and leafy greens. Higher price because the materials and system parts cost more.

How it connects

Keboon Assistant is the service. Keboon is the supporting platform.

The main product is the physical garden setup. Keboon can support it later with garden records, harvest updates, and optional extra produce listings.
