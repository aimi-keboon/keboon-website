About Keboon

Keboon is An innovative online marketplace connecting local producers with consumers, reducing food waste and promoting sustainable local food ecosystems.

Easy Marketplace

Buy and sell fresh, locally-grown produce with ease through our user-friendly platform.

Sustainable Living

Reduce food waste and support local farmers while enjoying fresh, organic produce.

Community Building

Connect with your neighbors and strengthen local food networks.

  • The Problem

    Excess Produce Waste

    Excess home-grown produce often goes to waste, despite efforts to share with neighbors. Not everyone has the time or network to effectively distribute their surplus.

  • Our Solution

    Keboon Marketplace

    Keboon is an innovative online marketplace designed to connect local producers with consumers, providing a platform to sell surplus home-grown produce and buy fresh, organic, locally-sourced products.

  • Our Mission

    Sustainable Local Food Ecosystem

    We're committed to reducing food waste, supporting local farmers and gardeners, promoting the consumption of fresh, organic produce, and strengthening community bonds through the sharing of locally-grown food.

  • Key Features

    Empowering Producers and Consumers

    Keboon offers a user-friendly platform for producers to easily list and sell surplus produce, while consumers can browse and buy fresh, local products. We're building a community of individuals passionate about sustainable, locally-grown food.
