What is Keboon?

Keboon is an innovative platform designed to transform local communities' interaction with their food systems. We connect home gardeners, urban farmers, and eco-conscious consumers in a vibrant marketplace.

Our mission goes beyond just creating an app – we're cultivating a movement towards sustainable, community-centered food practices that reduce waste and strengthen neighborhood bonds.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive through sustainable, locally-sourced food systems, reducing waste and fostering meaningful connections between neighbors.

Our Mission

Creating an innovative platform that seamlessly connects local producers with consumers, promoting sustainable living and strengthening community bonds.

The Challenge We're Solving

Addressing critical issues in our food systems

The Problems

Food Waste Crisis

Excess home-grown produce often goes to waste due to lack of efficient distribution channels.

Inefficient Distribution

Limited networks and time constraints prevent effective surplus produce sharing.

Disconnected Communities

Growing disconnect between local food producers and consumers.

Our Solutions

For Consumers

Access to fresh, organic, nearby produce with complete transparency about food sources.

For Producers

Monetize surplus produce, reduce waste, and connect with local community.

For Communities

Strengthen local economy and promote sustainable food networks.

Understanding our market segments

TAM
SAM
SOM

TAM - Total Available Market

Eco-conscious consumers, home gardeners, and urban farmers across Southeast Asia. RM 15 billion annual revenue potential.

SAM - Serviceable Available Market

Malaysian eco-conscious consumers and urban farmers. RM 500 million estimated annual revenue potential.

SOM - Serviceable Obtainable Market

Initial target of 1 million Malaysian consumers within first few years, aiming for RM 120 million annual revenue potential.
