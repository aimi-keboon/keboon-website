Our Vision: The Full Keboon Platform

Keboon will be a comprehensive marketplace connecting home gardeners, urban farmers, and eco-conscious consumers. Imagine a platform where your neighbor's surplus tomatoes are just a tap away, complete with AI-powered growing tips and community rewards.

But we're taking a strategic approach. Before building the full app, we're validating our market and building community trust through simpler, proven solutions.

Starting Here: Keboon Directory

Our current MVP is the Keboon Directory - a comprehensive database of local farms and producers. Think of it as the foundation of our ecosystem, helping consumers discover fresh, local produce sources.

This directory validates our core hypothesis: people want to connect with local food sources. Every farmer we onboard and every consumer we connect teaches us valuable lessons for the full platform.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive through sustainable, locally-sourced food systems, reducing waste and fostering meaningful connections between neighbors.

Our Mission

Building step-by-step from community directory to full marketplace, validating each piece before creating the complete local food ecosystem.
