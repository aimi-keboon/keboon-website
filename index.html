Keboon.

Connecting Neighbours, Sharing Freshness

An innovative online marketplace connecting local producers with consumers, reducing food waste and promoting sustainable local food ecosystems.

The Problem

Excess Produce Waste

Home-grown produce often goes to waste. Current solutions like sharing with neighbors are inefficient and limited in reach.

Disconnected Communities

Local producers struggle to connect with nearby consumers interested in fresh, organic produce. There's a lack of efficient platforms for hyperlocal produce exchange.

Economic Inefficiency

Small-scale producers lack avenues to monetize their surplus, missing out on potential income. Consumers often pay premium prices for organic produce in stores.

Sustainability Challenges

Current food distribution systems contribute to higher carbon footprints. There's a growing need for more sustainable, localized food networks.

Our Solution: Keboon Marketplace

User-Friendly Mobile App

Intuitive platform for easy listing and purchasing of local produce. Features include geolocation, secure payments, and community engagement tools.

Hyperlocal Focus

Connect producers with consumers in their immediate vicinity, reducing transportation needs and ensuring freshness.

Trust and Quality Assurance

Integrated review system and admin approval process to maintain high standards and build community trust.

Key Features:

  • Interactive map showing nearby produce locations
  • Date-organized product listings with detailed information
  • Integrated wallet system for secure and easy transactions
  • Admin approval process ensuring quality and trust
  • User profiles for both producers and consumers

Upcoming Features:

  • Rewards system for transactions
  • Planting scheduler for producers
  • Recycling integration for compostable waste
  • Extended categories for other home-produced items

Target Market

Keboon targets 3 million Malaysians across three primary segments:

1.5M

Home Gardeners

Small-scale farmers, home gardeners, and individuals with surplus home-grown produce.

Needs: Platform to sell excess produce, reduce waste, and earn additional income.

500K

Urban Farmers

Professional and part-time farmers in urban areas with access to community gardens.

Needs: Platform to market and sell produce directly to local consumers, connections with potential buyers.

2M

Eco-Conscious Consumers

Health-conscious individuals, families, and those who prefer organic, fresh, and locally-sourced food.

Needs: Access to fresh, organic, and locally-grown produce with clear understanding of food origin.

Market Trends:

  • Growing interest in organic and locally-sourced food
  • Increasing awareness of sustainable practices
  • Development of local food networks
  • Rising focus on food security and self-sufficiency
