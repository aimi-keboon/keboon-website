Keboon: Connecting Neighbours, Sharing Freshness

Introduction

Keboon is an innovative platform addressing the issue of excess home-grown produce going to waste. We connect local producers with consumers, ensuring no produce goes to waste and promoting local, organic food consumption.

Problem Statement

Issue

Excess home-grown produce often goes to waste.

Common Solution

Sharing with neighbours, but not everyone has the time or network to do so effectively.

Opportunity

Create a platform where local producers can sell their surplus, ensuring no produce goes to waste and promoting local, organic food consumption.

Solution: Keboon

An online marketplace connecting local producers with consumers.

Target Market

Keboon targets 3 million Malaysians:

Target Market Profile

Target Market Profile Needs Value Proposition
Home Gardeners (1.5 million) Small-scale farmers, home gardeners, and individuals with surplus home-grown produce. A platform to sell excess produce, reduce waste, and earn additional income. Keboon provides an easy-to-use marketplace where producers can monetize their surplus, connect with local buyers, and contribute to the local economy.
Urban Farmers (500,000) A mix of professional urbanites and part-time farmers focused on urban agriculture, primarily in urban areas with access to community gardens. A platform to market and sell produce directly to local consumers and connections with other urban farmers and potential buyers. Keboon provides a marketplace to reach local consumers, enhancing profitability and visibility. It supports their mission to contribute to urban food security and sustainability.
Eco-conscious Consumers (2 million) Health-conscious individuals, families, and those who prefer organic, fresh, and locally-sourced food. Access to fresh, organic, and locally-grown produce with a clear understanding of the food's origin. Keboon offers a convenient way to buy trusted, local produce directly from growers, often at more competitive prices than traditional grocery stores.
