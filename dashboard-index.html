How It Works

Getting started with Keboon Directory is simple. Follow these easy steps to connect with local farmers.

1

Browse Local Farms

Use our interactive map to discover farms near your location. Filter by products, farm size, and farming practices.

2

View Farm Profiles

Click on any farm to see detailed information including products, prices, farming methods, and contact details.

3

Connect & Purchase

Message farmers directly through our platform to ask questions, place orders, or arrange visits to the farm.

4

Enjoy Fresh Produce

Pick up your order or arrange delivery. Enjoy knowing exactly where your food comes from and how it was grown.
